our history

Claricent Company Ltd was established in 2016 with a vision to redefine excellence in construction and engineering services. From its inception, the company positioned itself as a forward-thinking enterprise, blending technical expertise with innovative solutions to meet the evolving demands of modern infrastructure. Starting as a modest firm, Claricent quickly earned recognition for its commitment to quality, safety, and sustainability. Over the years, it has expanded its portfolio to include civil engineering works, structural design, project management, and turnkey construction solutions. Each project undertaken reflects the company’s dedication to precision and reliability, making it a trusted partner for both private and public sector clients.

Driven by a culture of continuous improvement, Claricent has built a reputation not only for delivering projects on time and within budget but also for fostering strong client relationships. Today, the company stands as a dynamic player in the construction and engineering industry, with a proven track record of transforming ideas into enduring structures that support economic growth and community development.

our mission
